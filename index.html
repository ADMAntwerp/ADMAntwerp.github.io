Research Themes

Explainable AI

We develop methods that make black-box machine-learning decisions interpretable, including counterfactual explanations and rule-based approaches.

Responsible AI

Our work addresses fairness, accountability, and transparency in AI systems, ensuring data-driven decisions are ethical and unbiased.

Predictive Modeling

We build powerful predictive models from fine-grained behavioral data — payment, location, and browsing — across finance, healthcare, and beyond.
