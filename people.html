Principal Investigator

Prof. Dr. David Martens

Principal Investigator · Chair of ACRAI

David Martens is a professor at the University of Antwerp's Department of Engineering Management and chair of the Antwerp Centre on Responsible AI (ACRAI). His research focuses on explainable AI, counterfactual explanations, and building predictive models from fine-grained behavioral data across domains including finance and healthcare. He led the University of Antwerp to become the first in Flanders to offer a course on Data Science and Ethics.
