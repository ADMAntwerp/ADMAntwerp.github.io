Research Themes

Explainable AI

We develop methods that make black-box machine-learning decisions interpretable to humans — including counterfactual explanations, rule-based approaches, and feature-importance techniques.

Responsible AI

Our work addresses fairness, accountability, and transparency in AI systems, ensuring that data-driven decisions are ethical, unbiased, and legally sound.

Predictive Modeling

We build powerful predictive models from fine-grained behavioral data — payment histories, location traces, browsing activity — across finance, healthcare, and beyond.
